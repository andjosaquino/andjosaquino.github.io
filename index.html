ABOUT

How does my journey of soul-searching and angst begin?

Over the years, the prospects of my future adjusted like clockwork. Never have I ever been resolved in what type of person I desire to be in 20 years. To me, it is still a mystery even if I roll up my snuggie sleeves and lament away at my supposed triumphs.

Unlike my inconsistent aspirations, my fascination towards the digital medium and its effects in improving one's lifestyle never changed. Therefore, with interests business, psychology, and design, I decided to pursue computer science to complete my skillset towards all stages of app development.

Currently, I endure the harsh winters of Cornell University, drudging through the even harsher engineering curriculum. Despite my stark indifference towards physics and chemistry, engineering instilled a mentality and work ethic towards full-stack development.

Professionally, I operated as a freelance graphic designer since early highschool and currently serve as an art directon for school organizations; in addition, I recently completed a six month contract as a UI/UX Dev for a promising social networking app. On the side, I am a member, choreographer, and e-board member of a hip hop dance group, Breakfree.
